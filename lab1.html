A little information about cats
Cats one of the most popular home animal in the world.
There is a legend, that they can heal the ills of his owner.

There is a top 3 popular cats breed:

  • Exotic Shorthair
  • Ragdoll
  • Devon Rex

Female cats have two X chromosomes (XX), while a male cat has one X chromosome and one Y chromosome (XY).
Color in cats is mostly related to their sex because the gene causing color is on the X chromosome.

Because a male cat has only one X gene, if that X chromosome has the orange gene, he will be orange.
This means that if an orange female cat has a litter of kittens, all the males will be orange—regardless of their dad’s color.
But a female cat will only be orange if both of those X chromosomes carry the orange color, meaning both parents must be orange to create an orange female kitten.
